About The Game

Save The Sleeping Turtle is a fun physics-based puzzle game where you draw protective lines to save adorable sleeping turtles from dangerous bees, spikes, lava, magnets and many creative obstacles. Complete over one hundred handcrafted levels, unlock amazing turtle skins and enjoy a relaxing adventure full of challenges.
