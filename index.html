About The Game

Save The Sleeping Turtle is a relaxing physics puzzle game where you draw protective lines to defend sleeping turtles against dangerous bees, spikes, lava, magnets and many clever traps. Complete more than 100 handcrafted levels, unlock adorable turtle skins and challenge yourself with increasingly difficult puzzles.
