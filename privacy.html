Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Save The Sleeping Turtle ("the Game") collects, uses and protects your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address or phone number.

The Game may automatically receive anonymous technical information provided by Google services.

Advertising

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous information such as your device type, advertising identifier, approximate location and app interactions in order to provide and improve advertising services.

Google Play Games

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, Google may process your account information according to its own Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The Game may offer optional purchases such as Remove Ads through Google Play Billing. Payment information is securely processed by Google. The developer never receives your payment details.

Game Progress

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. We do not upload your saved data to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Save The Sleeping Turtle is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

The Game uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay statistics such as completed levels, failed levels, rewarded ads usage, tutorial progress, shop activity, and other gameplay events. These analytics help improve the game experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google Play Billing

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future changes will be published on this page.
